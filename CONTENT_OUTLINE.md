## Tone & Copy Review Checklist

Before any copy ships, verify:
- [ ] Headline leads with a verb or outcome noun — not a product name
- [ ] Every stat is sourced (PhenoTips internal data, user surveys, or named institution)
- [ ] No use of: "revolutionary", "game-changing", "cutting-edge", "seamless" (without proof)
- [ ] No exclamation marks
- [ ] No emoji
- [ ] Product name: always "PhenoTips" (never "Pheno Tips", "phenotips", "PT")
- [ ] Compliance acronyms formatted as: `HIPAA | GDPR | DSPT | SOC2 | CE+`
- [ ] HPO = Human Phenotype Ontology (expanded on first use per page)
- [ ] Second person for customer: "your patients", "your workflow", "your program"
- [ ] Third person for product: "PhenoTips captures…", "PhenoTips integrates…"
