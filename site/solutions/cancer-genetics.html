The challenge

Redundant data entry is costing your team hours every day.

Running a cancer risk assessment means re-entering family history into CanRisk, then IBIS, then documenting in the EHR. The pedigree gets redrawn from scratch at each visit. For a program seeing 200 patients per month, that adds up to hundreds of hours lost to duplicated effort — time that could be spent with patients.

How PhenoTips helps

One record. Every model. Zero re-entry.

Pre-Visit Questionnaire

Send a questionnaire. Receive a pedigree.

Patients complete a digital pre-visit questionnaire from home. PhenoTips uses their responses to automatically draw their family history pedigree — before the appointment begins. Your counselor opens the chart and the pedigree is already there.
