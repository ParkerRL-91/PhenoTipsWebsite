Health Systems

Unite every genetics team under one record.

Across academic medical centers, regional networks, and multi-site health systems, genomic data is fragmented. PhenoTips provides the infrastructure to standardize, connect, and scale genetics programs — without replacing what your teams already use.

Deployed across 100+ institutions on four continents
Book a Demo Explore the Platform
The challenge

Fragmented tools mean fragmented care.

Each site, each lab, each specialty team runs its own tools — and none of them talk to each other. Patients who move between locations have their history re-entered from scratch. Clinical directors have no visibility across teams. Without a shared record, scaling genomic care across a system is not possible.

How PhenoTips helps

Connect your entire genomics network — without replacing existing systems.

SMART on FHIR · Native EHR

Embedded directly in your EHR.

PhenoTips runs as a SMART on FHIR app inside Epic and Oracle Health (Cerner) — single sign-on, patient context handed in automatically, structured records returned to the EHR. The same FHIR-native interoperability extends to laboratory information systems and any standards-compliant external service.

Connect EPRs, lab hubs, and clinical teams across every site

HPO Standardization

Speak the same clinical language across every site.

With Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) capture built in, every genetics team at every site documents phenotypes the same way. That enables cross-site search, outcome comparison, and clinical decision support at a scale that siloed tools cannot provide.
