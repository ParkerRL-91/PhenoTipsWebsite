The challenge

The diagnostic odyssey is a data problem.

Phenotype data lives in free-text notes. Pedigrees are redrawn at every visit. Variant findings stay in the lab while the clinical team works in isolation. Without structured, connected data, the right answer takes years — and some patients never get there.

How PhenoTips helps

Four things your team can accomplish — starting day one.

Structured Phenotyping

Turn clinical notes into structured data — automatically.

PhenoTips concept recognition reads free-text notes and maps findings to Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) terms. Every symptom is captured, standardized, and searchable — no manual coding required.

Standardized phenotype capture across your entire team

Pedigree Maker

Build an inclusive, accurate pedigree in minutes.

PhenoTips supports all family structures — including non-traditional and multi-generational pedigrees. Patients complete a pre-visit questionnaire from home, and PhenoTips auto-draws the family history before the appointment begins.

Diagnostic Insights

Surface gene and diagnosis suggestions from the phenotype.

PhenoTips analyzes the full HPO phenotype profile to suggest differential diagnoses, relevant gene panels, and similar cases — pointing your team toward answers at the start of the workup, not years into it.
