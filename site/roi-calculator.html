Methodology

Where your savings come from.

PhenoTips delivers measurable value across three distinct streams. Each is grounded in published outcomes data and conservative clinical assumptions.

Clinical capacity

50% reduction in first-encounter time (pre-visit questionnaire auto-draws the pedigree). 50% reduction in risk assessment time (one pedigree runs CanRisk, Gail, IBIS, PREMM5, Tyrer-Cuzick). Reclaimed hours serve more patients — or cut staff cost.

Diagnostic accuracy

Structured HPO phenotyping improves first-time test selection. Teams order the right panel the first time — cutting an estimated 15% of duplicate tests, reflex orders, and follow-up rounds that result from incomplete phenotype data.

Shortened odyssey

Rare disease patients average 4–7 years to diagnosis and 8 specialists. PhenoTips concept recognition + differential suggestions compress that timeline — saving an estimated $8,000 per case in downstream specialist visits, unnecessary testing, and delayed therapy.

The uncountable value

And the outcomes money cannot measure.

Earlier diagnosis means earlier therapy, earlier family planning, and earlier closure for patients who have spent years searching for answers. Improved accuracy means fewer false leads, less patient anxiety, and more appropriate clinical care. Those outcomes do not appear on a spreadsheet — but they are the reason your program exists.
