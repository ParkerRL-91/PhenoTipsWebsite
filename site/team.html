How we work

Clinical-first. Genomics-fluent. Built to last.

Clinical-first

Most decisions involve a clinician. We design alongside genetic counselors, geneticists, and clinical lab leads — not in isolation.

Genomics-fluent

HPO. Pedigrees. Risk models. Variant interpretation. We speak the language because most of the team has worked in it.

Built to last

PhenoTips has been in continuous clinical use for over a decade. We make decisions that work for the next decade — not the next quarter.
