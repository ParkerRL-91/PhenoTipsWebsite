All features

Everything your program needs, built in.

Core Platform

Build the complete patient story.

Structured, curated, interoperable genomic data — from first appointment to final variant report. The complete Unified Genomics Workflow Solution.

Pedigree Maker

Draw accurate pedigrees in minutes.

Inclusive of all family structures. Auto-populated from patient questionnaires. Collaborative, exportable, and EHR-embedded. Rated best-in-class by 95%+ of users.

HPO Phenotyping

Capture every finding in a standard language.

Concept recognition maps free-text clinical notes to Human Phenotype Ontology (HPO) terms automatically. Every phenotype is searchable and comparable.

Pre-Visit Questionnaire

Patients arrive with their pedigree already drawn.

A digital questionnaire sent before the visit auto-generates the family history pedigree — saving 30+ minutes at intake and eliminating re-entry.

Cancer Risk Assessment

Run CanRisk, Gail, IBIS, PREMM5, and Tyrer-Cuzick simultaneously.

All major hereditary cancer risk models calculated from one shared pedigree. Side-by-side results. No re-entry. Saves approximately 30 minutes per assessment.

Variant Analysis

Connect phenotype to variant.

Review variant data in the full context of the patient's clinical record and HPO phenotype profile — supporting more confident clinical interpretation.

AI-Assisted Insights

Order the right test the first time.

AI-assisted test recommendation surfaces the most appropriate genomic test based on the patient's HPO phenotype profile, family history, and prior workup — reducing duplicate orders and re-tests.

Report Generation

Produce structured clinical reports in minutes.

Auto-generated reports formatted for patients, referring physicians, and the EHR — including pedigree, risk scores, and variant findings.

EHR Integration · SMART on FHIR

Work where you already work.

Embedded directly in your EHR as a SMART on FHIR app — Epic, Oracle Health (Cerner), and any HL7-FHIR compliant system. Single sign-on, patient context handed in automatically, structured records returned to the EHR. No custom middleware. No duplicate documentation.

Clinical Cloud

The full workflow — sized for smaller teams.

A lightweight, purpose-built version of PhenoTips for individual genetic counselors and small clinical teams. The same pedigree, phenotyping, risk assessment, and reporting tools — without the implementation overhead of an enterprise EHR integration. Up and running in days, not months.

Integrations

Connects to the systems your teams already use.

PhenoTips runs as a SMART on FHIR app inside Epic and Oracle Health (Cerner) — and exchanges data with any HL7-FHIR–compliant system. No custom middleware. No re-implementation.

Certifications

Certified for every regulatory context.

From HIPAA in North America to DSPT for NHS deployments — PhenoTips meets the security and compliance requirements of every major healthcare jurisdiction.
